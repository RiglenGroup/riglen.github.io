Challenges

Lack of Organizational Alignment

Adoption journey has already started and its not possible to realize the value of the cloud, due to a not integrated coordination and divided approach

Business Direction

Not having a clear direction and objectives to adopt more agile ways of working. Not sure which strategy (public cloud, private cloud, multi-cloud, hybrid) suits best for your organization

Organizational Change

Lack of understanding of the organizational transformation needed across people, processes, and technology to realize the benefits the cloud brings

Traditional IT Strategy

Outdated IT Strategy and lack of leadership support to defined success metrics criteria

Benefits

Understanding of Cloud Benefits

Highlighting the cloud benefits enables your organization to create application modernization roadmaps to bring new business values and competitive advantages

Impacts and Opportunities

Generating a common and clear understanding of the impacts and opportunities the cloud adoption journey brings to your organization

Clear Business Direction

Improve organizations' strategic alignment by having a clear business direction and expected business results, creating an effective communication mechanism

Executable Strategy

Enabling organizations to meet their vision of the cloud within the business goals, defining achievable timelines for a successful cloud adoption
