Strengthening European Presence and Advancing Long‑Term Growth

Zurich, Switzerland - {{ page.date | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Riglen Switzerland GmbH was officially born today in Zurich, Switzerland.

The management has announced today the official foundation of Riglen Switzerland GmbH, marking the establishment of the company’s first legal entity in Switzerland and a major milestone in Riglen’s European expansion strategy.

The launch of Riglen reflects the company’s commitment to building a strong, sustainable presence in one of Europe’s most trusted enterprise and innovation hubs. Switzerland’s reputation for regulatory excellence, stability, and global connectivity makes it a strategic location to support customers, partners, and stakeholders across Europe and beyond.

This milestone reinforces Riglen’s long‑term vision to deliver innovative, enterprise‑ready solutions while operating close to its customers and key markets.

Creating Value for Customers, Partners, and Investors

Riglen is committed to provide trusted, compliant, and scalable solutions for its customers. For partners, it opens new opportunities for collaboration within a stable and business‑friendly ecosystem designed for long‑term success.

From an investor perspective, the Swiss entity represents a strategic infrastructure investment, and supports Riglen’s ambitions for sustainable growth, operational maturity, and expansion into regulated and high‑value markets.

Building a Platform for Talent and Innovation

Switzerland’s strong pool of technical and professional talent will also play a key role in Riglen’s future. The new entity positions the company to attract, develop, and retain top talent, while fostering a culture of innovation, excellence, and accountability.

Riglen Switzerland GmbH will serve as a foundation for continued investment in people, partnerships, and technology—ensuring Riglen is well positioned to scale responsibly and deliver long‑term value to all stakeholders.

A Strategic Step Forward

The launch of Riglen Switzerland GmbH is a statement of intent. Riglen is committed to building a future‑ready organization, grounded in customer trust, strong governance, strong values, and continuous innovation.

As Riglen continues to grow its footprint in Europe, the company remains focused on what matters most: empowering customers with innovative solutions, supporting partners through collaboration, and creating sustainable value for the future.
