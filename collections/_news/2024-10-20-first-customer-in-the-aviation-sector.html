Riglen Strengthens Its Commitment to Innovation in the Public Sector

Zurich, Switzerland - {{ page.date | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

The company has signed its first contract in the aviation industry in Europe, marking a significant milestone in the company’s continued expansion and strategic positioning as a trusted enterprise partner in the public sector.

Riglen is proud to announce the signing of its first contract with a European governmental aviation agency, establishing the company’s entry into the public sector and defense industry. This marks a new chapter in the company’s growth reinforcing its position as a trusted provider of enterprise‑ready, mission‑critical solutions.

The agreement reflects Riglen’s proven ability to meet the high standards of security, reliability, and regulatory compliance required by governmental organizations. It also highlights the confidence placed in Riglen’s technology and expertise to support complex aviation environments operating at national and strategic levels.

For Riglen, this contract goes beyond commercial expansion. It represents a strong validation of our long‑term vision: to design and deliver innovative solutions that support critical infrastructure, enhance operational efficiency, and enable secure modernization across highly regulated industries.

As aviation authorities and defense institutions across Europe continue to accelerate digital transformation, Riglen is uniquely positioned to act as a reliable technology partner — combining innovation with an uncompromising focus on security, compliance, and operational excellence.

We see this milestone as the beginning of a broader journey — one where Riglen continues to invest, innovate, and deliver solutions that customers can trust in the most demanding environments.
