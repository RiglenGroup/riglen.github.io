Riglen Strengthens Its Position in Switzerland’s Public Sector and Defense Industry with New Government Contract

Zurich, Switzerland - {{ page.date | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Riglen is proud to announce the signing of a new contract with a Swiss governmental agency operating within the public sector. This agreement represents a significant milestone in Riglen’s growth journey and reinforces the company’s trusted role in delivering secure, innovative, and enterprise‑grade solutions.

The collaboration underscores the confidence placed in Riglen’s technological expertise, operational rigor, and long‑term commitment to supporting governmental institutions with solutions designed to meet the highest standards.

This contract further strengthens Riglen’s growing footprint in Switzerland and reaffirms its position as a trusted partner for government organizations seeking modern, secure, and future‑proof solutions. It also reflects Riglen’s broader vision of contributing to public institutions through responsible innovation and sustained collaboration.

As Riglen continues to expand its presence across regulated industries, the company remains dedicated to delivering measurable impact, cultivating long‑term partnerships, and setting new benchmarks for excellence in the public sector ecosystem.
