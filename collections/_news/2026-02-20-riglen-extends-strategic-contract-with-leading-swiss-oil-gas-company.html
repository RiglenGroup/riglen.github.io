A key milestone reinforcing Riglen’s position as a trusted partner in the energy, oil and utilities sector

Zurich, Switzerland - {{ page.date | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Riglen today announced the successful extension of its contract with a leading Swiss oil and gas company, marking a significant milestone in the company’s growth and long‑term positioning within the energy, oil and utilities industry.

The contract extension underscores the strength of the partnership and reflects continued confidence in Riglen’s ability to deliver innovative, high‑performance solutions that support operational excellence, efficiency, and resilience in an evolving energy landscape.

As part of the extended engagement, Riglen will continue to deliver tailored, enterprise‑grade solutions designed to enhance operational efficiency, support modernization initiatives, and address the complex challenges facing oil and gas organizations today. The collaboration also opens new opportunities for further innovation and long‑term value creation.

The agreement represents an important step forward in Riglen’s strategic roadmap and strengthens its presence in the Swiss and broader European energy market. By combining technological excellence with a deep understanding of industry‑specific requirements, Riglen remains focused on enabling sustainable, scalable, and future‑ready outcomes for its clients.
