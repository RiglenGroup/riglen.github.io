Riglen Signs First Major Contract with Swiss National Oil & Gas Company

Zurich, Switzerland - {{ page.date | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Riglen is proud to announce the signing of its first major contract with a national Oil & Gas company in Switzerland, marking a pivotal milestone in the company’s growth and market positioning within the Energy, Oil & Utilities industry.

This landmark agreement represents a significant step forward in Riglen’s strategic expansion and validates the company’s mission to deliver innovative, reliable, and future‑ready solutions for complex industrial environments. Partnering with a nationally recognized Oil & Gas organization underscores the trust placed in Riglen’s technical expertise, operational excellence, and commitment to long‑term value creation.

The engagement will focus on supporting critical initiatives across the energy value chain, leveraging Riglen’s capabilities to enhance efficiency, performance, and sustainability. Through this collaboration, Riglen further strengthens its role as a dependable partner for national and enterprise‑scale energy operators, addressing today’s operational challenges while preparing for tomorrow’s demands.

The Swiss energy market represents a critical hub for innovation, quality, and regulatory excellence. Riglen’s entry into this segment demonstrates its readiness to operate at the highest enterprise standards, offering solutions designed to drive resilience, innovation, and measurable business impact.

As Riglen continues to expand its footprint across the energy, oil, and utilities sector, this achievement serves as a strong foundation for future partnerships and reinforces the company’s commitment to supporting national and international energy leaders through technology, innovation, and trusted collaboration.
