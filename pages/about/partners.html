Growthing Together

Our partner network enables us to unlock new revenue streams, strengthen our competitive edge, and create meaningful business opportunities across industries and geographies.

By collaborating with best-in-class partners, we co-create innovative solutions that address complex challenges, drive digital transformation, and deliver exceptional value to our clients.

Partners Programs

Solutions Providers

Design, build, and implement complete solutions using our technologies. Taking a hands-on delivery role in architecting environments, integrating platforms, customizing deployments, and ensuring successful technical outcomes. These partners combine deep technical expertise with industry knowledge to deliver end‑to‑end solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Independent Software Vendors (ISVs)

Build, integrate, or extend software products that enhance our core capabilities. Their solutions plug into our ecosystem to deliver added functionality, automation, analytics, or industry-specific value. By aligning with Riglen Group's architecture and standards, they help create differentiated and scalable offerings that solve complex challenges and accelerate innovation.

Resellers and Consultants

Advise, recommend, and distribute our offerings without necessarily delivering or implementing the full solution. They focus on guiding clients through strategic decisions, aligning business goals with the right technologies, and facilitating procurement. Their strength lies in advisory services, market reach, and helping customers adopt the right Riglen Group capabilities.

Managed Services Providers (MSPs)

Operate, manage, and optimize our solutions on behalf of clients. They take responsibility for day-to-day operations, monitoring, security, and continuous improvement - ensuring environments remain reliable, compliant, and high-performing over time. Through fully managed or co-managed service models, they deliver long-term value and operational excellence across diverse industries and geographies.
