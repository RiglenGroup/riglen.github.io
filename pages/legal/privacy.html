Tracking technologies and Cookies

Cookies are small text files which are stored on the user browser when you visit a website. This makes your browsing experience as smooth as possible and they remember your preferences, avoiding to reinsert your preferences and details over again.

In order to optimize your experience, we use Google Analytics to ensure an optimal experience with the website. For this reason, at the time you visit our webiste, cookies are been installed and used from your computer. Without this information we won't be able to provide a tailored experience, this information will be only be used to improve your user experience and help you to find the information you are looking.

Riglen's website is using the following type of cookies for different purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary (Always Active): These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

  • Functionality: These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalisation. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies then some or all of these services may not function properly.

  • Tracking and Performance: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor its performance.

  • Targeting and Advertising: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store directly personal information, but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

Please be aware that these configurations are valid for pages managed by Riglen Group and when an external link is embeeded you should consider the new website cookies policies and settings instead.
